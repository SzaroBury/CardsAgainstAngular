Hello, players!

Single page application that let you play Cards Against Humanity with your friends.

Go to the Rooms page and join a room.
Or create your own room, choose the best settings, invite your friends and start playing.

Features:

  • Creating and customizing game rooms
  • Importing Cards and Sentences from a file
  • Playing the game
  • Basic log and chat
